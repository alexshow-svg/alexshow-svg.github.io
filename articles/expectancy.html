Expectancy: the only number that matters

Educational only. This is a concept, not a promise.

Plain definition

Expectancy is your average result per trade over many trades. It’s what happens after wins and losses mix together.

Simple formula

Expectancy ≈ (Win% × Avg Win) − (Loss% × Avg Loss)

Why win rate tricks people

  • You can win 70% of the time and still lose money if your losses are huge.
  • You can win 35% of the time and still profit if your wins are much bigger than losses.

Practical takeaway

Build systems that keep losses small and consistent. That’s what risk management is for.
